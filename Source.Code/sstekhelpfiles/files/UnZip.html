UnZip

UnZip        v6.0 (2009-04-20)

http://www.info-zip.org/UnZip.html

UnZip is an extraction utility for archives compressed in .zip format (also called "zipfiles"). Although highly compatible both with PKWARE's PKZIP and PKUNZIP utilities and with Info-ZIP's own Zip program, our primary objectives have been portability and non-MSDOS functionality.
